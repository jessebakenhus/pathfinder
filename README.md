# Pathfinder (WIP)

A smart app for planning running routes based on your preferences.

## Features

- **Route length**: Select your desired running distance
- **Elevation profile**: Filter for flat or hilly routes
- **Landscape type**: Find routes in forests, cities, parks, or nature
- **Display route**: Visualize your planned route on a map

## Usage

1. Open the app
2. Specify your preferences (length, difficulty, landscape)
3. Generate a route
4. Save or share your favorite routes
